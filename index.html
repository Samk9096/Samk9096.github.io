About Us

The Islamic Center of Anna and Melissa serves a diverse muslim population in Anna, Melissa and Van Alstyne. It was established in 2021 and started to function as a musallah in a storage unit. Alhumdulillah, we have been able to congregate for five prayers and have two jummah prayers. We are unable to accommodate our rapidly growing community in the current space and have grand plans for the for our muslim families that we can accomplish with your help. We wish to provide a place of peace, worship, friendship, and discussion for the entire community. We need a sacred space to organize events for our children and teach them the beautiful religion of Islam.
